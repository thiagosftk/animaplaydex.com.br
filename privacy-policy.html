Privacy Policy — Anima PlayDEX

Last updated: May 2026

The Anima Play DEX app respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data.

United States Privacy Rights

Residents of certain U.S. states may have additional privacy rights regarding their personal data, including the right to request information about data collection, usage, and sharing practices.

When applicable, the app will provide mechanisms for users to manage privacy and advertising preferences.

1. Information Collection

Anima PlayDEX may automatically collect limited information through third-party services used for monetization, advertising, and app functionality.

This information may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Ad interaction information
  • Approximate location data
  • Diagnostics and performance data

2. Advertising

Anima PlayDEX uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may use mobile identifiers, cookies, and similar technologies to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Users located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and other applicable regions may be asked to provide consent according to local privacy regulations, including the GDPR.

3. User Consent

When required, Anima PlayDEX displays a consent form provided by Google User Messaging Platform (UMP).

Users may accept, reject, or manage their advertising preferences.

4. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS devices, Anima PlayDEX may request tracking authorization through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

This permission may be used to provide personalized ads, when applicable.

5. Data Sharing

Anima PlayDEX does not sell users’ personal data.

Some information may be processed by third-party services, such as Google AdMob, for ad delivery, measurement, and personalization.

Learn more:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from apps and sites that use its services

6. Children’s Privacy

Anima PlayDEX is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

If improper collection of data from children is identified, appropriate measures will be taken to remove or limit such processing.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information processed by the app.

However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

The latest version will always be available on this page.
